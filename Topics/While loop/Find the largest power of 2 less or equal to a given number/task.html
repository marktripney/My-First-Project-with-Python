You have been given a positive integer n. Write a program that prints the largest power of 2 less than or equal to n. For example, if your input number is 25, your program needs to print 16 (which is 2 raised to the power of 4) because this is the largest number which is a power of 2 and is less than or equal to your input number.
Sample Input:
25

Sample Output:
16


Sample Input:
100

Sample Output:
64



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 5 seconds
